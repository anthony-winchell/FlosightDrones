Welcome to FloSight Drone Services

FloSight Drone Services is a veteran-owned business offering advanced aerial solutions for agricultural, residential, and industrial needs. Based in Central Florida, we are proud to bring high-quality, affordable drone technology to the communities we serve.

About

FloSight Drone Services is a veteran-owned business dedicated to providing high-quality, affordable drone solutions to clients across Central Florida. We specialize in:

  • drone Agricultural monitoring

    Optimize crop performance, detect issues early, and improve yields with our precise aerial imaging and data analysis.

  • drone Real estate imaging & inspections

    Showcase properties from breathtaking aerial perspectives or conduct safe, thorough inspections with minimal disruption.

  • drone Construction Progress Tracking

    Monitor large-scale projects from start to finish with scheduled aerial captures that document progress, improve reporting, and boost accountability.

  • drone Aerial mapping

    Get fast, accurate maps and 3D models for land surveying, construction planning, and asset management.

  • drone Roof & Infrastructure Inspections

    Perform close-range inspections of rooftops, cell towers, solar panels, and other hard-to-reach structures with zero risk to human workers.

  • drone Search & Rescue Support

    Provide real-time aerial assistance in emergency or remote search operations, especially in rural or difficult-to-access terrain.

  • drone Event & Promotional Footage

    Capture stunning aerial photos and cinematic video for promotional materials, marketing campaigns, or personal events.

Our mission is to make professsional drone services accessible to both businesses and individuals who value excellence, efficiency, and trust. Whether you're a farmer, realtor, contractor, or homeowner: we're here to help.
