About

FloSight Drone Services is a veteran owned company focusing on delivering quality and affordable drone services to the Central Florida area, specializing in agriculture monitoring, real estate imaging and inspections, as well as mapping. Our mission is to make professional drone services accessible to businesses and individuals who value excellence, efficiency, and trust. Our FAA-certified pilots use the latest drone technology to ensure safe, efficient, and accurate results for every project.
