Welcome to FloSight Drone Services

FloSight Drone Services is a veteran-owned business offering advanced aerial solutions for agricultural, residential, and industrial needs. Based in Central Florida, we are proud to bring high-quality, affordable drone technology to the communities we serve.

Services

  • drone Aerial Photography & Videography

    • Real estate marketing packages (photos, flyovers, lot outlines)
    Construction progress imagery and timelapse video
    Tourism, events, and promotional content capture
    Handheld Sony ZV-E10 camera used for hybrid ground/aerial shoots

  • drone Inspection/Survey Services

    • Structural roof and building inspections (residential/commercial)
    Volumetric measurements and stockpile assessments
    Thermal documentation
    Confined space FPV surveys for hard-to-access environments
    Post-disaster condition documentation (non-licensed assessments)

  • drone Community Outreach & Support

    • Free or low-cost emergency mapping after natural disasters
    Educational demos for schools and local STEM programs

    These services will be coming soon

  • drone Agricultural Prescriptions & Data Analytics

    • Generation of prescription maps for variable rate application
    Integration with platforms like Agremo and Pix4D
    Data overlays for agronomic decision-making
    Simplified, visual deliverables tailored for small-to-medium farms

  • drone Precision Agricultural Imaging

    • NDVI/NDRE crop health analysis + RGB imaging
    Field mapping for irrigation and soil management
    Canopy monitoring and plant count services
    Thermal imaging for livestock and water zones (observational use)

  • drone FPV Scouting & Confined Space Video

    • Dynamic fly-throughs for roof cavities, job site walkthroughs
    Pre-incident site overviews for public safety or insurance
    Close-proximity footage in GPS-denied or tight environments

Our mission is to make professional drone services accessible to businesses and individuals who value excellence, efficiency, and trust. Whether you're a farmer, realtor, contractor, or homeowner: we're here to help.
